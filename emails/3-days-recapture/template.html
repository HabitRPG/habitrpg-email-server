Greetings, adventurer!

Remember HabitRPG, the site that lets you treat your life like a game? We’ve noticed that you haven’t checked in for a few days. Don’t let your avatar die by neglecting your tasks!

If you did your tasks but are having trouble remembering to check in, we recommend setting HabitRPG as a bookmark or home page. Additionally, here’s an IFTTT Recipe that can remind you to check off your Dailies every night, and another that can remind you to check in each morning. (Both were created by talented users!) This wiki page also has tips on staying motivated!

Besides, there are lots of exciting things in your future. Unlocking new armor and weapons by level 2 is just the beginning. At level 4, you’ll unlock the drops system, with every completed task giving you a chance at eggs, potions, and food, which you can use to get pets and mounts!


Plus, at level 10 you’ll get the option to become a Warrior, Rogue, Mage, or Healer, all while making real-life progress!

Come join us! There are monsters to slay and lives to improve.
