Greetings, adventurer!

In honor of your second day with us, we wanted to share 5 easy tricks to help you get the most from HabitRPG.

  • Make HabitRPG.com a home page/bookmark.
    Our users tell us that this is their favorite method to help them remember to check in every day!
  • Set alarms.
    If you don’t complete your Dailies before the end of your day, they will hurt you! Set an alarm to remember to check them off.
  • Check out the website as well as the mobile app.
    Our website has more features, a tutorial, and lots of other great stuff! We’re working on rewriting our mobile apps to be native, but until then, take a peek at the website. Check out this screenshot for a sample:

  • Use the Data Display Tool
    Clicking the “Data” tab or going to data.habitrpg.com will show you how much damage your Dailies will cause, which To-Dos you forgot to tag, and more!
  • Join the Newbies Guild
    If you’ve got any questions, join the Newbies Guild! It’s a great place to ask questions.
If you use these tips and tricks, it’ll really help your experience in the land of Habitica.

Stay productive,
